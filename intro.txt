Introduction

Hello, welcome to ARToNaut.

The production of artworks is done to express ideas originating from a creator to an audience.

Naut is short for nautilus,
meaning related to water and ships.
You, the audience, are the guide for the ship, the artist,
in navigating the infinite waters of possibilities of how ideas are conveyed and understood
through the medium of artworks.

To begin guiding,
simply click on a part of the artwork you like.
